eSCREEN

Sans·Text

The pleasure of reading on screen

The first weight ESCREEN family, is a family sans-serif functional and displays legibility regardless of density. To reach this, we look at reference typefaces for screen such as Verdana, Lucida Grande or Phaedra.

The result is large x-height (ensuring that at low resolutions the forms are recognizable), open forms to improve readability, and simplification of geometric shapes to enhance its adaptation to hinting.
