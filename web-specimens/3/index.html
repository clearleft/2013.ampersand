About

Zagoris is a wedge serif type with an upright stress,
tall x height, and medium contrast, designed for
editorial usage. It has a text and a display version,
both influenced by archaic Romanian letter forms
used at the beginning of the 20th century. Zagoris
type is a work in progress and more weights are
presently under development.
